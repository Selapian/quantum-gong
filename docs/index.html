Quantum Gong is the first "Quantum Inbox," derived from the practice of Thich Nhat Hanh's Mindfulness Bell, which rings at Quantum Random times (about once every 40 minutes), using a free Quantum Random Number Generator (QRNG). The QRNG pushes a True Random notification event, all at the same time, to everyone connected to the app. When we each receive the "gong," we all practice Mindfulness, or sit and meditate, to cultivate ourselves!

Because websites cannot reliably push alerts while the screen is closed, it is necessary to install "Quantum Gong" as a Native App. Android works natively. On iOS (Apple), press the three dots, then Press "Share", and scroll down to "Add to Home Screen." Then Open 'Quantum Gong' like a native app from your Home Screen. If you are on Desktop, please use Google Chrome, install the App, then open "Quantum Gong" from the Desktop.

iPhone Installation

1. Tap the ... then Share button at the bottom of Safari.

2. Scroll down (or click See More) and Select "Add to Home Screen".
